The Kwans have discovered them and a quick act of diplomacy fails after Lemor-Ixa takes over control of their ship by jamming their controls. Lemor-Ixa demands that Quelatune promise to deport the slaves. Robert and Tiffany tell him to refuse. Rebekah unplugs the main communications device which will, yes, prevent their controls from being hijacked but would also prevent their ship from landing.
